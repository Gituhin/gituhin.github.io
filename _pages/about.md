Before my master's I worked as a software manager at ICICI bank (Techonology group) in Mumbai, India. I also interned in the data science group at [Udaan.com](https://udaan.com/) mentored by [Pranjal Singh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pranjalsingh/).

I obtained my dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Civil Engineering and specialization in artificial intelligence and applications from [IIT Kharagpur](https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/), India in 2024. I am into research because I am interested in solving math since [Regional Mathematics Olympiad 2016](https://olympiads.hbcse.tifr.res.in/olympiads/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/CBSE.pdf).
